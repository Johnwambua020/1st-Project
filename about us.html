About us

Tervan inn hotel was built in 2009, this upscale hotel is a 50-minute walk from the National Museums of Kenya and 10km from Uhuru Park. The elegant rooms and suites come with flat-screen TVs, Wi-Fi, and tea and coffeemakers, along with safes. Some feature verandas. Suites add living rooms, and some have private terraces. There's a chic restaurant, a casual eatery with a terrace, and a high-end wine bar. A sophisticated lounge offers high tea. Other amenities include a heated outdoor pool, a fitness center. Meeting and event space is available, including a ballroom. Breakfast is offered.
